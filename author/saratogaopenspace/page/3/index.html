Town of Saratoga Election Get out and VOTE on Tuesday November 7, 2017

Write-In Vote John Cashin

John Cashin as a write-in for:
Town of Saratoga Supervisor
and
All Town Board Councilman open positions.

John Cashin is a voice for our community.
If you believe the elected town board has not been attentive to your concerns about the direction of our Town, send them a message by writing in John Cashin on your ballot on Election Day.

Attention. Important Planning Board Meeting on Oct 25 2017. Please Attend.

On Wednesday October 25, 2017 at 730 pm at Saratoga Town Hall the Town of Saratoga planning board convenes a public hearing on the Witt development proposal. This public hearing is to determine whether the SEQR process requires the developer to conduct a full Environmental Impact Statement for this proposed development.
WE NEED YOU TO ATTEND AND DEMAND A DECISION REQUIRING THE FULL EIS!
