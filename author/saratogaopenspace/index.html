Stillwater Town Board Meeting on Thursday, February 15th at 7:00 p.m

The Stillwater Town Board will be taking up two development applications this Thursday, February 15th at 7:00 p.m. at the Stillwater Town Hall, 881 Hudson Ave.

The White Sulphur Springs PDD and Winding Brook PDD are slated to be discussed. Both fall within the Saratoga Lake watershed, and coupled with the Town of Saratoga development process, pose a risk and threat to the lake watershed. The Winding Brook PDD is to include 19 condominium buildings with 4 units per building, the White Sulphur Springs subdivision seeks approval for 21 single family home lots.
