Who We Are

Our Mission

Saratoga Open Space is a community organization dedicated to protecting the natural character and open spaces that make our town special. We monitor development proposals, advocate for responsible growth, and work to preserve the rural character of Saratoga.

Community Focused

We believe that engaged citizens make the difference in preserving our community's character. Through education, advocacy, and active participation in local government, we work to ensure that development serves the community's best interests.

Transparency

We keep residents informed about development proposals, planning board meetings, and town decisions that affect our community. Knowledge is power, and an informed community is a protected community.
